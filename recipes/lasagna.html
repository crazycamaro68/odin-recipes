Lasagna Recipe

This dish is perfect if your looking for a italian dish. The needed ingredients are as followed.

    Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Mushrooms
    Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Lay one layer of pasta
  3. Spread a layer of sauce over noodles
  4. Sprinkle in mushrooms as you like
  5. Repeat steps into pan full
  6. Baked for 40 minutes
